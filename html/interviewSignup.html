The Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago podcast series aims to bring together objective commentary from Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago experts to better inform the general public on the state of economic and monetary policy. Moreover, this podcast series aims to demistify the wide range of innitiatives and work being conducted at the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago through narratives and the sharing of work experiences. The aim is to foster a better understanding of what the Federal Reserve Bank of Chicago does, and how its employees work together to ensure the success of the institution.

To be considered for a future podcast episode, please fill out the form below and we will be in touch with you soon!
